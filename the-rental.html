The Rental.

From shaggy blue carpet to brand-new-everything. 🏚️🔨✨

Built 1925 Purchased 2020 Cincinnati, Ohio Status ✅ Complete
The Story
"Built in 1925, this rugged time machine with shaggy-blue carpet
and smoke-stained walls needed more than a fresh coat of paint."

In 2020, we purchased The Rental from our older neighbor turned friend. We gave this home the same love it gave to many families before us — foundational repairs, replacing walls, and a brand-new-everything gave us our rapid-start into real estate.
